What happens when Superheroes go out drinking together?

Players work their way through a deck of cards with varying effects, bonuses, powers, and blunders - drinking as they go.

Classic Would You Rather game. Definitely NSFW! Read the cards aloud and give some time for deliberation, guilt-tripping and arguing.
On the count of 3 everyone votes for their chosen card.
Vote by holding up one finger for the first card read out loud or two fingers for the second card read out.
Whichever card has the MOST votes, each player who voted for that card must drink - everyone else is safe.
In the case of a tie everyone drinks (sorry, not sorry).
